Chicken Chili

Description

This is a low sodium, low-calorie option for chili. The recipe can be made as spicy as you want by adding more jalepenos or a touch of cayenne.

Try topping with cheddar cheese, cilantro, avocado, or greek yogurt.

Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Roasted Chicken
  • 3 Cups Chicken Broth
  • 2 Cans Great Northern Beans
  • 1 Can Diced Chilis
  • 1 Jalepeno, chopped
  • Cloves of garlic
  • 1 TBSP Cumin
  • 1 TBSP Chili Powder
  • 1/2 TSP Oregano
  • 1/2 Small Yellow Onion, chopped

Steps

  1. Chop onion, garlic, and jalepeno
  2. Saute onion until translucent
  3. Add garlic and jalepenos, saute 1 min
  4. Add diced chilis and spices, stir 30 seconds
  5. Add chicken, stir 30 seconds
  6. Add beans(1/2 can smashed), broth, and stir
  7. Cook to taste, about 30 minutes, salt and pepper as needed
  8. Add cayenne if desired
  9. Dish out chili, top with cheese, greek yogurt, and avocado
